NET/JRF Coaching

National Eligibility Test is one of the major qualifying exams for aspiring teachers and research fellows. On behalf of UGC, National Testing Agency conducts the exam twice a year for the teaching in Indian colleges and universities. This test is also for granting Junior Research Fellowship to researchers in Indian universities.

HiG EDUCAMPUS offers coaching for both Paper1 and Paper2 COMMERCE. HiG has a specialized faculty line up entirely for UGC NET Preparation program and our classes are designed in a blended approach where both online and offline way of learning are clubbed. We also provide continuous mentoring for the students who are aiming to achieve their best positions in their career.

Paper 1

Paper 1 is common for all arts and social sciences courses and the syllabus includes Teaching Aptitude, Research Aptitude, Comprehension, Communication, Mathematical Reasoning and Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, Data Interpretation, Information and Communication Technology, People Development and Environment and Higher Education System. Paper 1 has a total score of 100 marks.

K-TET

Kerala Teachers Eligibility Test (KTET) is mandatory for the post of primary level teachers in the state of Kerala. KTET has four categories taking from the Lower Primary Level to High school Level.
